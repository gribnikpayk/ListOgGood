Shopping list! helps people to plan and manage their grocery shopping.
Your Shopping List stays with you everywhere you go and you'll have it on-hand once you're ready to go grocery shopping.

Our Features:
- Multiple shopping lists
- Save time by grouping items into categories
- Easily enter items in your shopping lists
- Share lists by text or email
- Shopping list! is optimized for smartphones, tablets and desktop
- Add product photos to the items on the shopping list, so you always buy the right brand
